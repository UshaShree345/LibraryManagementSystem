Objective

The primary objective of a library is to provide the right information at the right time in the right form to its users. To meet this objective, libraries have to provide access to information irrespective of their form, format and location.

Activities

It helps librarian to keep track of the library information.It mainly focus on basic operations in a library, like adding new books, and updating the information of books, searching books and facility to issue and return books.
